Equine Myofunctional Therapy (EMT)

What is EMT?

EMT is the therapeutic application of massage techniques to improve muscle function, mobility, and relaxation in horses. It is also known as equine sports massage, equine myotherapy, or horse massage.

How can EMT help my horse?

EMT stimulates the body's natural healing processes, providing benefits such as:

  • Pain relief
  • Reduced muscle fatigue & tension
  • Increased relaxation
  • Improved circulation & blood flow
  • Enhanced posture & range of motion
  • Decreased inflammation & prevention of adhesions
  • Better muscle tone & athletic ability

After an EMT session, Yvette may recommend stretching exercises or a polework program to maximize results, improve proprioception, strength, and muscle tone.

  • Follow-up sessions: $80 AUD per horse


Saddle Fitting

What is Saddle Fitting?

Saddle fitting involves a thorough assessment of your current saddle to ensure optimal fit and comfort for both horse and rider. This includes evaluating:

  • How the saddle sits on the horse
  • Potential pressure points or discomfort
  • Rider balance & weight distribution

If needed and suitable for the type of saddle: adjustments such as gullet widening/changing or wool flocking can be made (additional costs apply). If a saddle cannot be adjusted to fit, Yvette will provide recommendations.

For your session, please have all saddles needing assessment ready.

How can Saddle Fitting help my horse?

  • Prevents discomfort & injuries from poor saddle fit
  • Enhances relaxation & willingness to work
  • Reduces anxiety when ridden
  • Decreased anxiety when ridden
  • Improves performance through better gear fit
  • Enhances communication through seat aids

Pricing

  • Saddle Fitting Assessment: $150 AUD per horse.
  • Additional adjustments (e.g., gullet changes, wool flocking) priced separately


Bit Fitting

What is Bit Fitting?

Bit fitting involves a detailed assessment of your horses current bit, mouth shape, and comfort level to find the most suitable option.

  • Examination of the horses oral conformation
  • Trialing different bits to assess response & comfort
  • Ridden (preferred) or groundwork assessment (please have your horse tacked up if a ridden assessment is booked)

How can Bit Fitting Help My Horse?

  • Prevents discomfort or injury from incorrect bits
  • Encourages relaxation & acceptance of the bit
  • Improves communication & rein aids
  • Reduces anxiety & resistance
  • Enhances performance with a better-fitting bit

After an assessment, Yvette may recommend suitable bits from Bit Bank Australia's range if you don't already have an appropriate option.

Pricing

  • Bit Fitting Assessment: $100 AUD per horse.
