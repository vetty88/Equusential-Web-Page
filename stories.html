Duke

Duke is a long-term client of Equusential, he was an original case study! Duke had suffered a serious floating accident months earlier and also had developed suspected stringhalt. He was a mess! It has been a long journey of dedication, care and commitment to get him to the blue-ribbon working condition of this past year. To learn more about Duke's despair to domination journey please see his blog post!

Project Two

Yvette's own "heart horse" Squirrel is the reason that Equusential began. Squirrel was suffering significant back pain and associated muscle damage/ scarring. Undoing this damage and building Squirrel up to be better in health with each passing year is Yvette's key motivator. Squirrel's journey to a healthy back and regular updates on progress can be found on our blog. Also check out our social media as she is a familiar face!
