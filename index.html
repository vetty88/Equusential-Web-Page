About Equusential Therapies

Started in 2016, Equusential Therapies' Yvette Waller brings Equine Myofunctional Therapy and Bit Fitting services to the East Gippsland/Gippsland area of Victoria, Australia

Having completed studies at the National College of Traditional Medicine and with a loyal client base Yvette's practice includes the study and assessment of the horse's muscles and the application of a variety of massage techniques. Termed Equine Myofunctional Therapy or “EMT.”

Yvette has also completed accredited training with Charmae Bell of Bit Bank Australia to become a bit fitting specialist. Yvette works closely with Bit Bank Australia to find the best brand, type size and style of bit for horses and riders in Gippsland.

If you are curious about if EMT or Bit Fitting is suitable for your horse, please have a browse of the materials on this site, check out our Facebook page, and feel free to contact Yvette.
