About Equusential Therapies

Started in 2017, Equusential Therapies' Yvette Waller brings Equine Myofunctional Therapy to the East Gippsland/Gippsland area of Victoria, Australia

Having completed studies at the National College of Traditional Medicine and with a loyal client base Yvette's practice includes the study and assessment of the horse's muscles and the application of a variety of massage techniques. Termed Equine Myofunctional Therapy or “EMT.” Following an EMT treatment Yvette will often recommend a program to get the best result from bodywork: to improve the horse's proprioreception, strength and muscle tone.

If you are curious about if EMT is suitable for your horse, please have a browse of the materials on this site, check out our Facebook page, and feel free to contact us!
