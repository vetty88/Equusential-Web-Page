Why Should I Book?

Equine Myofunctional Therapy (EMT) is a sports massage for the horse! Benefits to the horse are similar to those for humans and include pain relief, better muscle mobility, relaxation and flexibility.

Properly fitted tack ensures your horse is as comfortable as possible in their riding gear

Saddle Fitting involves an assessment of the current fit of your riding saddle and tack. Correctly fitted saddlery improves the performance and comfort of your horse.

Bit-fitting is a ridden or groundwork assessment with you and your horse to help find the best type of bit for your horse!

Yvette is a qualified Equine Myofunctional Therapist, trained Saddle Fitter and an accredited Bit-Fitter, insured and registered. Book with confidence that your horse is in experienced and knowledgeable hands!
