Why Should I Book?

Give your horse the care and comfort they deserve!

Equine Myofunctional Therapy (EMT) is a specialised sports massage for horses, offering benefits similar to those of human massage. These include pain relief, improved muscle mobility, enhanced relaxation, and increased flexibilityâ€”helping your horse perform and feel their best.

Saddle Fitting ensures that your horses tack is correctly adjusted for maximum comfort and performance. A properly fitted saddle and tack reduce discomfort and enhance movement, making your ride smoother and more enjoyable.

Bit Fitting is a ridden or groundwork assessment with you and your horse to help find the best type of bit for your horse!
