Why Should I Book?

Equine Myofunctional Therapy (EMT) is sports massage for the horse! Benefits to the horse are similar to those for humans and can include pain relief, better muscle mobility, relaxation and flexibility.

Bit fitting is a ridden or ground work assessment with you and your horse to help find the best type size and style of bit for your horse!

Yvette is qualified in Equine Myofunctional Therapy, Accredited in Bit Fitting, insured and registered, you can book with confidence that your horses is in experienced and knowledgeable hands!

If you are curious about if EMT or Bit Fitting is suitable for your horse, please have a browse of the materials on this site, check out our social media or contact Yvette!
